Learn Magazine

An addition to Learn.co’s blogging feature that allows students to publish blog posts written on the platform to a magazine where fellow students can read their posts.

Project Goal

Provide a beginner friendly space for web development students to practice their technical writing and share their blog posts.

Discovering the Magazine

We wanted the magazine feature to be easily discoverable. I added a right rail to the existing blogging dashboard with a CTA to the open the magazine. I also added a feed of the most recent student blog posts from the magazine to help pique interest

Sketches

During the initial brainstorm of the magazine feature we considered ways for users to find the content that they would find the most engaging. We thought of allowing users to be able to filter posts by topic or having a featured posts section. The Magazine

I worked on a few iterations of layouts for the feed of blog posts that make up the Learn magazine. We ultimately decided on a random grid of varying card sizes. Posts on the larger cards also include the lesson the user was on when they published the post for a little more context.
